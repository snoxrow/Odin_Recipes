Burger Recipe

Description

This page is going to walk you through the process of making a dellicious lasagna dish that can be done in under 50 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Lettuce
  • Buns
  • Quarter pound of ground beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. First, we start boiling the pasta in a separate bowl
  2. Meanwhile, get the tomato sauce cooking and add spices as you desire
  3. Start cooking the ground beef in a separate pan and add a whole diced onion as well as the other Vegetables
  4. Finally, put everything together, start layering the sauce then the meat then the pasta and repeat in that order until you are done!
