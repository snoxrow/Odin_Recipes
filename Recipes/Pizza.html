Pizza Recipe

Description

This page is going to walk you through the process of making a dellicious pizza dish that can be done in under 4 hours!

Time: Takes about 3 hours!

Serving size: 8 Servings

Nutrition Info

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Cut pieces of Vegetables

Steps

  1. First, we start making our dough which can be found in another page, the dough should be made and let to rest for 2-3 hours prior or overnight!
  2. Start Making the pizza sauce, put your tomato sauce in a sauce pan along with olive oil, preferred spices and let simmer for 20 minutes or until reduced
  3. Start layering your pizza with the dough, then sauce base then top it with whatever you want
  4. Finally, place in an oven that has been pre-heated to 260 C, and leave for around 5mins and then you're done!
